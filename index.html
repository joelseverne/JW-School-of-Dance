JW School of Dance

Hi there!
I'm Jess and I proudly bring to you my company
'JW School of Dance'.

My first introduction to dance was at an early age when I attended a disco dance (freestyle) class at the age of 8 and I have been hooked ever since!

After competing for many years at championship status there was a natural progression for me to begin teaching. I ran my own weekly sessions from the age of 14 and then at 18 went on to study 'Dance' at University where by I graduated with a 1st Class B.A. (Hons) Degree.

I have gained a wealth of experience working with people of all ages & abilities over the years. My passion is to enable people to express themselves and ultimately enrich their lives through the medium of dance.
